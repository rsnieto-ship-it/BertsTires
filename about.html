About Bert's Tires

30+ years of tire expertise, now serving Houston

Meet the Founder

Bert's Tires was founded by Beto Levario, a tire industry veteran with over 30 years of experience. Originally from El Paso, Texas, Beto brought his expertise to Houston to serve the growing community with quality tires at unbeatable prices.

A Family Legacy in Tires

Tires run in Beto's blood. His father built a career in the tire business, and all of his siblings work in the industry as well. This family legacy means Beto grew up learning everything there is to know about tires - from sourcing and selection to proper installation and maintenance. When you work with Bert's Tires, you're getting generations of tire expertise.

Our Commitment to You

Beto built Bert's Tires on two core principles: exceptional customer service and the best prices around. He believes you shouldn't have to choose between quality and affordability. With three decades of industry connections and know-how, Beto sources premium tires at low prices and passes those savings directly to you.

Trailer Tire Specialists

While we carry tires for all vehicles, we've become Houston's go-to experts for trailer tires. Whether you have a utility trailer, boat trailer, RV, or commercial trailer, we have the knowledge and inventory to get you the right tires at the right price.
